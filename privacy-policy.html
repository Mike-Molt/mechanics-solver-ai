Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Mechanics Solver AI ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data.

Data We Collect

API Key: When you enter your Hugging Face API key, it is stored locally on your device only. We do not have access to your API key and it is never transmitted to us.

Questions: When you submit a statics question, the text you enter is sent to Hugging Face's servers for AI processing. We do not store or have access to your questions.

How Your Data Is Used

  • Your API key is used solely to authenticate requests to Hugging Face's AI service
  • Your questions are processed by Hugging Face's AI to generate solutions
  • We do not use your data for any other purpose

Third-Party Services

The App uses Hugging Face's inference API to process your questions. When you submit a question, it is sent to Hugging Face's servers. Please review Hugging Face's Privacy Policy for information on how they handle data.

Data Storage

  • Your API key is stored securely on your device using encrypted storage
  • Questions are not stored - they are sent directly to Hugging Face and discarded after processing
  • We do not maintain any servers or databases that store your information

Data Deletion

You can delete your stored API key at any time by using the "Reset API Key" button in the App. Uninstalling the App will also remove all locally stored data.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except as necessary to provide the App's functionality (sending questions to Hugging Face for processing).

Children's Privacy

The App is intended for educational purposes and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
